Mahabaleshwar Another popular hill station that attracts people from not just Maharashtra but other parts of the country too is Mahabaleshwar. It is as known for its natural vistas as it is for its strawberry farms. Located in the Western Ghats, the winding roads make for a beautiful long drive. On the way, you can also stop at Panchgani and then move on to Mahabaleshwar.
